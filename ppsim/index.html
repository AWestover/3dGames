Predator prey simulation

Alek Westover, highly esteemed dog of theland

Basic Idea:

I want to see what happens with some animals that must compete for food

Implementational details:

food will regenerate every eating cycle if an animal becomes too hungry it will either consume food if food is availiable or die of starvation if food is not availiable. Hopefully this will impose logistic growth on the population ie initially exponential growth and then food can't sustain the population in between feeding cycles animals hunger will increase and animals may reproduce

Simulation neglects:

this simulation assumes food is instant gratification for a single animal also this simulation neglects predators other special things and other users
